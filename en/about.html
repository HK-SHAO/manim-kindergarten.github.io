Manim Kindergarten is a organization established by BiliBili video creators and manim enthusiasts who use manim to make popular science videos in China.

It was founded on November 28, 2019, and was officially established on GitHub under the organization name "manim-kindergarten" on March 15, 2020.

The video projects of our members are mainly published on BiliBili, and related codes are uploaded and maintained on GitHub.

The organization currently contains about 500 members and 68 BiliBili video creators. The video subjects are mainly popular science types, distributing in many aspects such as mathematics, computer science, physics, chemistry, social science and so on.


We look forward to creating more and better popular science videos together. Welcome anyone who love manim to join us.
